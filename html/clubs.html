CASPER

Casper (at Verykoko) is a reference point for Thessaloniki's nightlife. For many it’s the most popular night club in the city with the best DJ's and live acts! It plays mainly Greek trending songs as well as some old Greek hits! It’s one of the biggest clubs in Thessaloniki since there are 3 floors in it. The staff are very kind, and they will do everything to satisfy you.

J'ADORE

J’adore is the other “landmark” of the city. There are mainly young people cause of the student parties! There are also people from all over the world cause of the music that plays here. DJs prefer hip-hop, trap and pop music that matches perfectly with the audience. Many events take place in J’adore like “Paradox” in which there is a live performance with professional dancers.

Manhattan

Manhattan sky terrace literally raised clubbing to a higher ground! The most beautiful rooftop in town transformed night clubbing into an unforgettable experience. With different kinds of music and the city’s view from above it is a unique destination! It is open only in the hot months because it is in the open air. Manhattan is not a huge club, but you can dance and drink as much as you want although its size.

Nhaos

Maybe the most popular nightclub right now in Thessaloniki, Nhaos is the place you have always dreamed of. With the most famous DJs and Music producers of the city Nhaos have weekly lives with the hottest names of the industry. Nhaos has something unique that will make you feel cozy and comfortable. The best place to drink and dance with your friends.
